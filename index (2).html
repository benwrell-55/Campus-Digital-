The Process

From Application
to Career-Ready

We've designed a clear path so you always know what's next.

01

Apply Online

Fill out our short application form — takes less than 5 minutes. No prior experience required for most tracks.

02

Get Enrolled

Our team reviews your application and reaches out within 2 working days to confirm your place and share onboarding details.

03

Choose Your Mode

Attend in-person at our campus in Nairobi or join every session online via our learning platform — or switch between both.

04

Learn, Build, Present

Complete hands-on projects, receive mentorship, and build a portfolio you can show employers.

05

Graduate & Get Placed

Earn your certificate, join our alumni network, and access job placement support and internship connections.
